TimerVault Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 18, 2026

TimerVault ("the app") is a countdown timer and stopwatch app. Your privacy matters, and the app is built to need none of your personal information.

Data We Collect

None. TimerVault does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data. There are no user accounts, no sign-in, no analytics, no advertising, and no third-party tracking or SDKs.

Data Stored on Your Device

Your timers, settings, and preferences are stored only on your device using standard iOS storage. This information never leaves your device and is not accessible to us. Deleting the app removes this data.

Purchases

TimerVault offers a one-time in-app purchase (TimerVault Pro) processed entirely by Apple through the App Store. Apple handles the transaction; we do not receive your payment details or any personal information about you. Purchase management and refunds are handled through your Apple account.

Children's Privacy

TimerVault is rated 4+ and is safe for all ages. Because the app collects no data, it collects no data from children.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted on this page with a new effective date.
