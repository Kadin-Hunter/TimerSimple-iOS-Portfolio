TimerVault Support

One-tap timers for workouts, focus, and everything you time daily.

Getting started

Tap any timer on the main screen to start it instantly. Use the Quick Start timer at the top for a one-tap countdown, or tap the stopwatch to time anything on the fly.

Adding your own timers

Tap the "+" tile to create a custom timer with your own name, duration, and style. Free includes one custom timer; TimerVault Pro unlocks unlimited custom timers.

TimerVault Pro

TimerVault Pro is a one-time purchase that unlocks unlimited custom timers, editing any timer, full customization (colors, fonts, sounds, vibration, screen flash), and all exclusive designs. Open the paywall from the Pro badge in the header or when adding an extra timer.

Restoring a purchase

Already bought Pro on another device or reinstalled the app? Open the Pro screen and tap Restore Purchase.

Alerts

When a timer ends, TimerVault can play a sound, vibrate, and flash the screen. Adjust these in a timer's settings or in the app's global settings.
